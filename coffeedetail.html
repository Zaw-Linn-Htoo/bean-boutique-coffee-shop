Double Roast Coffee Beans

For those wanting a richer, heavy-bodied and gutsier cup of coffee. Expect varying intensities of spice, nuts and berry notes. These coffee beans are roasted darker until they are luxuriously oily and therefore produce a smoky finish in the overall intense taste. It is a traditional coffee in some European cultures, and you may need additional sugar to balance the intense flavour. Suitable for: Seasoned coffee drinkers. This is not a beginner's blend!

Flavour Rating: Strong 4
Roast Rating: Dark
